      Responsibilities

      • Implement frontend functionalities using pure JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.
      • Translate UX/UI designs from Figma into responsive, pixel-perfect frontend implementations, ensuring design fidelity and usability across devices.
      • Optimize applications for performance, scalability, and security.
      • Design, implement, and perform tests to ensure application functionality, performance, responsiveness, and security.
      • Collaborate with designers, developers, and stakeholders to deliver high-quality solutions.
      • Debug and troubleshoot technical issues across multiple browsers and devices.
      • Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code following best practices.
      • Read and edit other people's old code.
      • Stay up to date with emerging technologies and industry trends.

      Skill Requirements

      • High-level proficiency in modern CSS, including advanced layout techniques such as CSS Grid, Flexbox, and responsive design principles, with the ability to create complex, adaptive interfaces across a wide range of devices and screen sizes.
      • Experience with CSS preprocessors (SCSS, SASS) and modern tooling.
      • Strong knowledge of cross-browser compatibility and performance optimization.
      • Ability to develop interactive JavaScript-based games and web applications (preferred).

      Required Experience

      • 3+ years of experience as a Frontend Developer.
      • Experience with CSS preprocessors (SCSS, SASS) and modern tooling.

      Required Education And Qualifications

      • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology or related fields.

      Benefits

      • Premium Health Insurance as well as a yearly health check-up will be provided for official staff.
      • Insurances fully provided including Social Insurance, Health Insurance and Unemployment Insurance.
      • Regular training, as well as participation in various special programs and courses offered by the headquarters.
      • Discount up to 90% on English tuition fees for staff and close relatives.
